Engagment Levels

Inform

To provide patient with balanced and objective information to assist them in understanding the problem, alternatives, oppurtunities, and/or behavior changes

Consult

To develop personalized treatment for patient based on patient's real time feedback and analysis of population health data.

Collaborate

To partner with the patient and research communities to identify the preferred solution to effect outcome.

Empower

To place final decision-making in the hands of the patient and provides ultimate support system for patient on daily basis.
